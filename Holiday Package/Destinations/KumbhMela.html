Kumbh Mela Highlights

What makes our Kumbh Mela packages special

Spiritual Significance

The Kumbh Mela is considered one of the most sacred pilgrimages in Hinduism. According to Hindu mythology, drops of the nectar of immortality (amrit) fell at four places where the Kumbh Mela is held: Prayagraj, Haridwar, Ujjain, and Nashik. Our packages include guidance on the spiritual significance and mythology behind this grand event.

Royal Bathing (Shahi Snan)

Experience the most auspicious bathing days when millions gather to take a dip in the sacred waters at the confluence of the Ganges, Yamuna, and mythical Saraswati rivers. Our packages are timed to coincide with these special occasions, with dedicated viewing areas and guidance.

Sadhu Darshan

Meet the holy men and women of India who gather at the Kumbh Mela. Many sadhus belong to ancient akharas (Hindu monastic orders) and only appear in public during the Kumbh. Our guides will help you navigate these interactions with respect and understanding.

Comfortable Accommodations

Stay in well-appointed tents or luxury accommodations based on your package selection. All our accommodations include clean bathrooms, comfortable bedding, electricity, and security to ensure your stay is comfortable despite the massive gathering.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our Kumbh Mela packages

When is the next Kumbh Mela in Prayagraj?
The next Kumbh Mela in Prayagraj (Allahabad) is scheduled for 2025. The exact dates will be determined based on astrological calculations and will be announced closer to the event.
What is included in the packages?
Our packages typically include accommodation, meals, guided tours of the Kumbh Mela grounds, attendance at key ceremonies, transportation from designated pickup points, and assistance from experienced guides throughout your stay. Specific inclusions vary by package.
How crowded will it be during Kumbh Mela?
The Kumbh Mela is the world's largest gathering of people. Millions of pilgrims attend, especially on the main bathing days. Our packages are designed to provide comfortable experiences despite the crowds, with strategic accommodations and guided navigation through the festival.
Is the Kumbh Mela suitable for foreign tourists?
Yes, the Kumbh Mela welcomes visitors from all over the world. Our packages include English-speaking guides familiar with addressing the needs and questions of international visitors. We provide cultural context and ensure you have a respectful and enriching experience.
What should I pack for Kumbh Mela?
We recommend packing modest clothing (shoulders and knees covered), comfortable walking shoes, a hat, sunscreen, insect repellent, any required medications, a water bottle, and a small bag for daily excursions. A detailed packing list will be provided upon booking.
